HTML TAGS
Heading (h1-h6 bolds and enlarges the text)
        <h1> </h1>
<h2> </h2>


Image (attributes an image)
        <img> </img>
        Attributes: src, width, height, framborder


Links (anchor tag, links to another internet location)
        <a> </a>
        Attributes: href=”https://website.com”


Lists (defines ordered/unordered list of items)
        <ol> </ol>
        <ul> </ul>


List Item (defines list of ordered/ unordered items)
        <li> </li>


Paragraph (specifies a paragraph, creates new line)
        <p> </p>
