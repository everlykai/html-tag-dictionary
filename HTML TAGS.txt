HTML TAGS
a- Links (anchor tag, links to another internet location)
        <a> </a>
        Attributes: href=”https://website.com”
        
div - division (defines a section, used as a container to style or perform tasks)
        <div> </div>

h1- Heading (h1-h6 bolds and enlarges the text)
        <h1> </h1>
        <h2> </h2>

img- Image (attributes an image)
        <img>
        Attributes: src, width, height, framborder

li- List Item (defines items in list of ordered/ unordered items)
        <li> </li>
        
ol/ul- Lists (defines ordered/unordered list of items)
        <ol> </ol>
        <ul> </ul>
        
P- Paragraph (specifies a paragraph, creates new line)
        <p> </p>

style- (placed in headings, used to change appearance)
        style= ""
        atrributes: font-style, color, etc.
